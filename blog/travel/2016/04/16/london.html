London

Apr 16, 2016

London is super cool. I really really enjoyed our weekend there. Being around English folks is strangely comforting after being in Amsterdam for 1.5 years, and getting beer in proper full pints is freaking awesome. Had loads of cask ale! The city is more walkable than I expected, and we covered most of what we saw on foot. So much good food, coffee, beer, shopping, events, and interesting work avaialble here. 2 days really isn’t enough time to get familiar, but I can see us moving here for at least a some amount of time before we leave Europe. Definitely worth the visit.

 

Cheers!
