Milan

Oct 2, 2016

The first stop on our whirlwind tour de Italia was Milano. This place is massive. It’s known for it’s shopping which there’s certainly no shortage of. The Duomo is a fascinating marble church, the size and detail is mind-bending. Milan is also the home to Da Vinci’s Last Supper, it’s quite a feeling to be standing in front of such a monumental piece of artwork.

Salute!
