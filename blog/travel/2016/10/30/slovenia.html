Slovenia

Oct 30, 2016

Krysten and I spent an absolutely epic weekend in Slovenia. This place is so underrated! What an incredible hidden gem in Europe. One of the most beautiful places we’ve seen. It has 50% dense forest cover over the entire country and is super lush. It’s quiet and sleepy, even in the main city Ljubljana. We covered it by car which we’d highly recommend. Going in October worked out great, it was a bit drizzly, but that full gamut of leaves and those misty hills are hard to beat.

Na zdravje!
