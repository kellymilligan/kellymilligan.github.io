Naples and Pompeii

Oct 6, 2016

Our final stop in what was busy 2 weeks in Italy. Napoli is quite different to the other cities we saw. It’s a bit more rough around the edges, but still has it’s own charm. Mount Vesuvius offers a pretty neat view, and Pompeii is absolutely incredible. It’s worth coming to Italy just to see Pompeii, what a treasure of human history. We unfortunately didn’t have enough time to see all of it! We’ll be back.

Salute!
