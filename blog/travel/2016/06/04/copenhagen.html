Copenhagen!

Jun 4, 2016

I love Copenhagen. We spent 4 days here and had a great time. So much great food, beer, and coffee culture - perfect for us! The weather was gorgeous while we were there. Danish design is super cool, we were lucky enough to be there during an event called 3 days of design. Many of the design studios and showrooms opened their doors and we could see their workspaces and talk to the designers. Danish folk are lovely, friendly, polite, happy, and the city is tidy. The Freetown is an entertaining semi-dystopian experience, no photos allowed so you’ll need to experience it for yourself ;)

Skål!
