Scotland

Jun 3, 2017

Four awesome days travelling through rural Scotland. Started in Edinburgh then drove west. Stopped in at Loch Lomond and Luss, had a gorgeous sunny drive through Glen Coe where we stayed the night. Day two we hit Glenfinnan and various parts of Skye before stopping in Portree. Day 3 more of Skye before heading south to Newtonmore, staying the night in Kingussie. Final day was spent slowly making our way back down to Edinburgh, and a mandatory stop at Dalwhinnie.
