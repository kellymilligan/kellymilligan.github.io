Day trip to Brussels!

Jan 18, 2015

A little trip to Belgium, what a treat. Frietes, waffles, beers and chocolate, seriously neat! Seriously though, these things are pretty over done in Brussels… ;)

Then there’s the Manneken Pis statue… This tiny statue is flaunted as one of the most impressive things Brussels has to offer. It’s kinda bizarre, this tiny bronze statue of a pissing boy, that attracts crowds, and gets plastered all over the city. I don’t really get it!

The most fascinating thing for me was all of the old architecture. There’s amazing old buildings in Brussels, and oh my god there’s a lot of them! The town square is surrounded by some of the most beautiful old buildings I’ve seen so far.

Other highlights included an antique market with about 100 people selling some incredibly old artifacts - but in a casual Saturday market fashion, a music museum with a dense array of different musical instruments and hills (what a novelty coming from Amsterdam!).

On the way back to the train I stopped at an awesome bar called Moeder Lambic. It’s a lambic bar, with such an impressive range of traditional and contemporary sour beer. I had a Tilquin on tap and it was exemplary. What an extraordinary place, I really look forward to spending more time here!
