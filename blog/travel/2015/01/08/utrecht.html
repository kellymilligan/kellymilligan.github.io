Visiting Utrecht

Jan 8, 2015

Utrecht is another city close to Amsterdam. It’s about 40m south on the Metro. It’s a Student town, with more modern stuff knocking about than Amsterdam central or Haarlem. It’s got a great atmosphere and culture - some really interesting stuff going on, especially with music.

It’s not without historical charm though, it has these really unique lower-level canals throughout. There’s basement level building access on the canal level.

There’s an impressive tower called Domtoren and pretty sweet old church. The tower is the tallest in The Netherlands, unfortunately it was closed the day I visited but you can access the top normally.

There’s also a pretty rad museum dedicated to self-playing instruments called Museum Speelklok. It’s got a collection of creepy-as-fuck old musical character pieces - most of them horrifically racist. Interesting old disc and cylinder players, and pretty excessive “party organs” which can be enormous. Unfortunately they don’t show any of their collection pieces in action, just on display. I understand wanting to preserve these oddities, but being able to actually see a self-playing instrument in action would really be a unique experience.
