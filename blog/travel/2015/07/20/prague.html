Prague

Jul 20, 2015

My folks came to visit us here in Europe :) We spent a week travelling around central Europe with them, Prague > Vienna > Budapest. Other destinations to follow soon!

Prague was cool: A bit rough around the edges, but it’s really big, loads of Czech pilsner around, everything is super cheap (24Kč = €1), heat wave put the temp at 34c! Traditional absinthe experience is great, awesome food festival a wee way away on the metro.

Proost!
