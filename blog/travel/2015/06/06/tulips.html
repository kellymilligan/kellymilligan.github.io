Dutch tulips (and Keukenhof)

Jun 6, 2015

What’s The Netherlands without Tulips!? Spring is the season, and we had a pretty cool time checking em’ out.

A friend we’ve made since being here is a hobby pilot. He took us up for a ride to see the tulip fields from the air! Thanks to Krysten for these wonderful photos, I was in the front-seat taking off (and getting badly motion sick…).

The following weekend we decided to head south to Keukenhof, the famous, massive tulip gardens they have here. It’s a pretty impressive, and extremely well maintained collection of floral treats. If you’re not a fan of floral photography, you may want to move on to the next post…

Was a fun experience, and I’d recommend it if you’re visiting in spring :)

Proost!
