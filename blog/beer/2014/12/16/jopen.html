Jopen and other Haarlem beer

Dec 16, 2014

While kickin around Haarlem I also went to visit the Jopen Brewery. Jopen beers are really decent, and they make a bunch of different styles - I especially like their Rye Pale Ale.

The brewhouse is surrounded by their awesome brewpub called the Jopen Kerk (Jopen Church). Really nice atmosphere and decor, high ceilings, beautifully arranged place. The Brewhouse is a fully automatic system, and with blingy polished copper accents.

I met up with Robert the head brewer thanks to an introduction by Niels from Schippers. Robert is a seriously passionate brewer, great dude, and also owns Uiltje (The Little Owl). He brews the Jopen & Uiltje beers in parallel on the Jopen system. He’s also opening a Uiltje specific bar in Haarlem next year. Generally the Uiltje beers are much more experimental than the Jopen beers, including some seriously bangin IPA’s and Barrel Aged Imperial Stouts.

To round out my day trip I stopped off at an excellent bottlestore called Melgers Wijn en Dranken and grabbed a few Uiltje beers. Their selection is dammmmn fine.

Finally I spent a couple of hours at a great beer and whiskey bar called Cafe Briljant. Awesome small slice of craft beer/spirit culture here in The Netherlands :)
